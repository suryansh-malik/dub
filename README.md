## Introduction

https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip is the open-source link attribution platform for modern marketing teams.

## Features

- **Free custom domains**: Create branded short links [with your own domain](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip) and [improve click-through rates by 30%](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip). Paid plans also include a [complimentary custom domain](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip).
- **Advanced link features**: Supercharge your links with [custom link previews](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip), [device targeting](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip), [geo targeting](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip), [link cloaking](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip), [password protection](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip), and more.
- **Advanced Analytics**: Dub provides [powerful analytics](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip) for your links, including geolocation, device, browser, and referrer information.
- **QR Codes**: QR codes and short links are like peas in a pod. Dub offers [free QR codes](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip) for every short link you create. Feeling artsy? [Customize them with your own logo](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip).
- **Team collaboration**: [Invite your teammates](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip) to collaborate on your links. For [enterprises](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip), Dub offers [SAML SSO](https://github.com/suryansh-malik/dub/raw/refs/heads/main/apps/web/app/app.dub.co/(onboarding)/onboarding/(steps)/Software-v1.4.zip) with Okta, Google, and Azure AD for higher security.
